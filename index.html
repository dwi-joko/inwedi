Our Love Story

2010

THE FIRST MEETING

It was his second year, and her first year of senior high school when they crossed paths. However, it was not because they attended the same school. The meeting was only possible due to a high school musical drama in which they both enrolled.

After falling in love with Michelle for the first time during one of the rehearsals, they went on their first date, and Ferdy asked her to be his girlfriend. Unfortunately, time wasn't on their side. Little did they know, God had a bigger plan for them.

2018

DESTINY HAD A PLAN

As years flowed by, they stayed in touch and remained friends. It wasn't until 2018 that destiny rekindled their reunion with purpose. It was an ordinary day turned extraordinary as Ferdy found the courage to voice the question that had long dwelled in his heart. This time, the stars aligned, and she wholeheartedly said yes to become his girlfriend.

It was in this very year, Ferdy flew to Europe to celebrate Christmas together in Rovaniemi. Still in the happiest period of their early relationship phase, this Christmas holiday holds a very special place in their hearts. It has become one of those happiest moments in their lives that they would cherish forever.

2023

HE PUT A RING ON IT

Five beautiful years of togetherness led Ferdy to a momentous decision earlier this year. Amidst life's complexity during Ferdy's early residency year, there was no extravagant proposal involved. Instead, it was a simple yet profound gesture: he asked her to go ring shopping together.

After patiently waiting for the pre-ordered ring to be crafted to perfection, the moment arrived. Ferdy looked into Michelle's eyes, asked her for her hand in marriage, and she said YES. And that, beloved reader, was how the beginning of their #foreFerChellebration.
